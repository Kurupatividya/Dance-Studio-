About Us

Welcome to RK Studio, where the magic of dance comes to life! Since our establishment in 2000, we have been dedicated to nurturing the art of dance and providing a platform for passionate individuals to express themselves through movement.

Our Journey

Established in 2000, we embarked on a mission to make the world a better place through the power of dance. Our founder, Savitri Garu, a true visionary and a passionate dancer, laid the foundation of our studio with a dream of sharing the joy of dance with the world. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant and inclusive community of dancers who are committed to our mission.
